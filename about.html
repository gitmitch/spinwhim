Spin Whim!

X

Spin Whim gives you instant turn-by-turn biking directions to a public park near you.

Spin Whim finds your location (or you can move the biker around on the map to set it), then plots all public parks a certain distance away.

Click or tap on a park to instantly get biking turn-by-turn directions. If you're on an iPhone or iPad with Google Maps installed, you'll be taken directly to the Google Maps app.

Can't decide? Click the "Random" button to be taken to a random park on the map.

In the mood for a longer or shorter ride? Drag the sliders at the bottom of the screen to change the riding area, or click directly on one of the numbers to change it. You didn't see a slider? Click or tap on the hamburger icon to reveal it.
